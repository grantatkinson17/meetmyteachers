COMM 2074: Intro To Sports Media

Professor

The professor for this Intro to Sports Media class is Bill Roth. Mr. Roth is one of the coolest people I have ever met, and he is an outstanding professor. He also has extensive expereince in the field, which I will describe in the next section.

Education

Bill Roth got his bachelor's degree from Syracuse University in Broadcast Journalism.

Interests

Mr. Roth is one of the more interesting professors here at Virginia Tech. He was the play by play announcer for Virginia Tech Athletics for 27 years before moving to California in 2015. After one year, he came back to Blacksburg and helped establish a strong Sports Media Concentration within the Multimedia Journalism major. As a professor of practice, Mr. Roth still serves as a play-by-play braodcaster for select games on ESPN on top of teaching.

Class Description

At the begininng of the year, Professor Roth described this class as the Golden Coral of Sports Journalism classes. What he means by this is that we cover all kinds of different topic in Sports Journalism. These topics include Sports Analytics, Marketing, Writing, Broadcasting, and much more.

Opinion

I really love this class. Since Sports Writing is pretty much what I want to do as a job, that probably makes sense. I am very passionate about this, so it makes it not even seem like work when I have to do homework or anything for this class. It is also very applicable to my desired job of Sports Writer and/or Broadcaster. I find that it is much easier to do assignments if they are direcrly related to what I want to do in the future.

Expected Grade

I currently have an A in this class. I think there is a chance that I could bring it up to an A plus, but I will be happy with just a regualr A.
