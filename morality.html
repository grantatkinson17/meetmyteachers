Class Description

In this class, we discuss general principles of Philosophy such as the moral statuses of actions. Then, we apply that knowledge to specific hot button topics such as abortion and capital punishment. We also examine articles from well-known philosophers and dissect their arguments about abortion, capital punishment, or other subjects.

Opinion

I really like this class. At first I was just taking it in order to fulfill a credit requirement, but I have really become interested in the subject matter. I am a prety logic-heavy person, so the dissecting arguments and those types of things generally come pretty easy to me. I also have strong opinions on issues, and I like to discuss those (in a respectful manner.)

Expected Grade

I currently have an A in this class. Professor Novack also drops the lowest three to four quizzes, so that will be helpful. I expect to keep an A for my final grade.
